Built for students, by students.

Created by a team of students, Rasper Maths was designed to combat the current issues plaguing the market of maths homework platforms, such as gimmicks, high prices and other anti-consumer practices. Hence, all features are tested by a group of students, to ensure enjoyment (and consequently productivity), is as high as possible.

FAQs

"What is Rasper Maths?"

Rasper Maths is a mathematics homework and learning platform for schools created to combat the issues surrounding most homework platforms these days. We understand that many schools face financial constraints that prevent them from purchasing quality learning platforms that enable students to do the best they can do.

"How much does it cost?"

"What makes Rasper Maths different from other platforms?"

Rasper Maths is developed by a team of students which allows us to better understand the needs of students and what makes an effective and engaging learning platform. It is also free - we understand the financial constraints faced by many schools who may not be able to afford quality homework platforms.
